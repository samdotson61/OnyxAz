How to connect
--------------
1. Install OnyxAz: double-click "Install OnyxAz.cmd" (Windows) and pick your vault.
   Then enable OnyxAz in Obsidian -> Settings -> Community plugins.
2. On the OnyxAz setup screen, choose "SSO for Teams".
3. Click "Paste setup document to autofill" and paste this whole message.
   (The organization URL and client ID fill in automatically.)
4. Enter your work email address and click "Sign in with Microsoft".
5. Complete the browser sign-in, then pick your repository.

Need help? Contact your IT team.
